About Us

Wajiki

Wajiki is a non government organization that established in 2009 and officially registered on 23rd June 2014, the organization located at Makumbusho in Dar es salaam region that focuses on bringing changes to the society about the women and children. Our vision is to build a platform that fights for women and children rights in Tanzania, also providing education to the society about the consequences of sexual corruption, gender oppression, how to overcome those challenges and empowering women economically.

Safari salama bila rushwa ya ngono is a campaign that was conducted with the aim of providing education to the society about sexual corruption so as to help women and children especially girls to reach their dreams.
